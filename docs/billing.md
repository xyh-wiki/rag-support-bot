# Billing

## Plans and pricing

CloudPeak offers three plans:

- **Starter** — $12/month. 1 project, 2 GB RAM, community support.
- **Pro** — $49/month. 10 projects, 8 GB RAM, email support with 24h response time.
- **Business** — $199/month. Unlimited projects, 32 GB RAM, priority support with 4h response time, SSO.

All plans are billed monthly by default. Annual billing gives a 20% discount.

## Refund policy

We offer a full refund within 14 days of your first payment, no questions asked.
After 14 days, refunds are prorated for annual plans only; monthly plans are not refundable but can be cancelled anytime and remain active until the end of the billing period.

To request a refund, email billing@cloudpeak.example with your account email and invoice number. Refunds are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.

## Changing plans

You can upgrade at any time from Dashboard → Billing → Change plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and the price difference is prorated.
Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Payment methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Business plan customers can pay by wire transfer on annual billing. We do not accept cryptocurrency.

## Invoices and tax

Invoices are generated automatically each billing cycle and available under Dashboard → Billing → Invoices. You can add a VAT number and company address; they appear on all future invoices. We cannot regenerate past invoices with changed tax details.
