# Billing

## Plans and limits

Starter includes 5 seats, 1 workspace, 1 GB of document storage, and 60 API requests per minute. Pro includes 25 seats, 5 workspaces, 20 GB of document storage, and 300 API requests per minute. Business includes custom seats, unlimited workspaces, 1 TB of document storage, and 2,000 API requests per minute.

## Trial period

New workspaces receive a 14-day Pro trial. The trial does not require a credit card. At the end of the trial, the workspace moves to Starter unless a paid plan is selected.

## Refund policy

Refund requests are accepted within 14 days of the first payment. Annual plans may receive prorated refunds after the first 14 days when required by contract. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time but remain active until the end of the billing period.

To request a refund, email billing@northwind.example with the workspace slug, invoice number, and billing contact email.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, the billing contact receives an email immediately. The system retries payment on day 1, day 3, and day 7. If all retries fail, the workspace becomes read-only until the invoice is paid.

## Invoices and tax details

Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing cycle. Tax ID, billing address, and purchase order number can be updated from Settings -> Billing. Changes apply to future invoices only.
